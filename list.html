Baskin Robbins :

Baskin-Robbins is an American multinational chain of ice cream andbr

cake speciality shops owned by Inspire Brands. Baskin-Robbins was founded

in 1945 by Burt Baskin and Irv Robbins in Glendale, California. Its current

headquarters are in Canton, Massachusetts, and shared with sibling brand Dunkin

Donuts.
